Welcome to Site Valkyrie

Site Valkyrie is one of the most secretive and secure SCP Foundation facilities, located deep underground and hidden from the public eye. This site is responsible for containing some of the most dangerous SCP anomalies known to the Foundation, including those with Keter and Thaumiel classifications.

Site Valkyrie has been operational since 19██, serving as a primary research and containment hub. It features the latest in anomalous detection technology, including an advanced AI system and bio-secure entry points. Only personnel with proper clearance are allowed access beyond the entry levels.

Recent improvements to Site Valkyrie include the integration of automated defenses, reinforced containment cells, and enhanced security protocols, ensuring the safety of Foundation staff and preventing any unauthorized access.

Research

Site Valkyrie hosts ongoing research into various SCP anomalies, focusing on better containment, neutralization, and understanding of their effects. Researchers are reminded to submit their reports and findings through the proper channels.

Incident Reports

Incident reports regarding containment breaches, MTF deployment, and classified operations are available only to personnel with Level 4 clearance. Unauthorized access to these reports is strictly forbidden.

Contact

To report an issue, breach, or anomaly within Site Valkyrie, please contact the Site Director immediately using the encrypted communication channels. Unauthorized communication attempts will be intercepted and monitored by Foundation AI systems.
